Gym Tracker App is a web application designed to help users track their workouts across different exercise machines. Users can log workout details such as distance and time, view workout history, manage personal profiles, and calculate BMI based on their weight. The frontend is built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, providing a clean, animated interface. The backend uses Node.js and Express to handle user data and workout records. This simple, responsive app is ideal for anyone wanting to monitor their gym sessions effectively.
